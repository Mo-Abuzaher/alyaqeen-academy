Tahfeez

تحفيظ القرآن

Memorization of the Holy Quran. Our qualified teachers guide students through systematic memorization with proper tajweed, helping them commit the entire Quran to memory with understanding and reverence.

  • One-on-one sessions
  • Structured memorization plan
  • Revision & retention support

Reading

قراءة القرآن

Learn to read the Quran with correct pronunciation and fluency. Our reading program focuses on proper recitation, tajweed rules, and building confidence in reading the Holy Book.

  • Tajweed-focused instruction
  • Fluency building
  • All levels welcome

Noorani Qaida

القاعدة النورانية

Perfect for beginners. Our teachers introduce the Noorani Qaida—a foundational booklet that teaches Arabic phonetics, the alphabet, and basic pronunciation rules. The essential first step for every Quran learner.

  • Arabic alphabet mastery
  • Phonetics & pronunciation
  • Beginner-friendly pace
